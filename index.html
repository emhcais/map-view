About This Map

This interactive map visualizes North African Captives in the Mediterranean Region and Europe from 1600 to 1690. Click on the branch to popup details about each individual. It includes each individual's name, when available, their baptismal name, place of captivity as the location, the current Pope's name, and the source.

  • Use the timeline to explore change over time.
  • Filter by Pope, Region, or Location.
  • Click on the location to zoom and view details.
